Built a classifier that distinguishes real audio from AI-generated. Extracted MEL Spectrogram from audio and created a balanced dataset. Compared various architectures like `AlexNet`, `MobileNet`, and `ResNet` against a simpler CNN. Achieved a maximum accuracy of **99.7%** using early stopping and **5-fold cross-validation**.
